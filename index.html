Who the hell we are?

We are company called Waste Processing Facilities. Our aim is to take care of waste after you take it to bin. We sort waste into categories depending of material that waste is being made. Then we process it. Processing type depends of category:

  • Plastics: shreddering, melting, refining into petrochemicals, asphalts, construction materials;
  • Metals: shreddering, melting(aluminum and light metals);
  • Wood: composting, burning, grinding;
  • Paper: refining, burning;
  • Organics: burning, composting, refining for chemicals and fuels;

..and so on and so forth

How do we take out trash?

We take out trash by cargo electric vehicles depending of type and quantity of trash you have.
